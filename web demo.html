HOUSE OF BRILLIANCE

House of Brilliance is a division of an importer and exporter of wholesale diamonds and fine jewelry that has been one the most influential organizations in shaping the diamond market in the Dallas metroplex. In addition, House of Brilliance will create custom pieces, offer their clients the option to build their own rings from an elegant selection of styles, or purchase ready manufactured fine jewelry.

For this project, we worked directly with husband and wife owners, Dahlia and Gil Abramov. Working through each step of our process, we created a professional yet elegant, feminine brand direction to appeal to their target audience. We also created a hand drawn illustration for the logo design to give it a unique touch while still aligning with the elegant feel that they wanted. For the website design, we worked alongside photographer Terri Glanger to style the jewelry pieces accordingly for each website page and overall created a design that would represent the client online in the most professional, sophisticated way possible.
